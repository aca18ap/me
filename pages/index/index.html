Officially British

After over seven years in the United Kingdom, on 27/01 I officially became a British Citizen!

Parliament Lobby Day - Banning Cages

On the 21st of October 2025, I was part of a mass lobby day to advocate for the end of farrowing crates and enriched cages for hens and saws. Here is a photo with my MP Olivia Blake, who was very supportive of the matter and helped us getting hold of the DEFRA ministers that could make this happen. A few months after the lobby day, government announced that the ban will be included in the latest animal welfare strategy, a huge win for animals!

Shredding keys at Wild Experience Fest in Romania

I had a blast playing the keyboard in the middle of the Transylvanian mountains at RockFest! We had a great week-long trip across Romania, from Bucharest we drove north for hours through the wilderness. Along the way we also got to stop at Dracula's castle!
